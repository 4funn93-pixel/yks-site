Burnout is not a badge of honor. It is a systems failure.

The operators who are still excited about their work in year 5 and year 8 have built **antifragile** personal operating systems.

Key principles from the [Precision Mindset](/guides/precision-mindset) and [Solopreneur OS](/guides/solopreneur-os) guides:

- The 90-minute deep work blocks (protected ruthlessly)
- Weekly “energy audit” review (not just task review)
- Decision debt elimination using pre-made trees
- Quarterly 3-day offsites (even if you never leave your city)

The guides contain the full rituals and Notion templates.

Protect your spark. The business depends on it.
