## The Brutal Truth About 3D Print Photos on Etsy

Most shops lose sales in the first 0.6 seconds because their hero image looks like it was taken on a kitchen table with overhead lights.

The top 3% of 3D print shops on Etsy share one trait: their photography looks like it came from a $2k studio — even when it was shot on an iPhone 14 with two $19 LED panels.

This post breaks down the exact system.

(Full 31-page version with templates and presets is available in [The 3D Print Photography Playbook](/guides/3d-print-photography-playbook).)

## The 5 Signature Setups (Start Here)

1. **Ghost Light** (our signature) — Dramatic side key + deep negative fill. Makes prints look expensive and mysterious.
2. **Clean Museum** — Pure white for technical/miniature products.
3. **Lifestyle Kitchen / Desk** — The one that actually converts browsers into buyers.
4. **Macro Texture** — Prove quality.
5. **Collection Group Shot** — Perfect for bundles.

Full diagrams, phone camera settings (ProRAW vs HEIF), and exact light positions are in the paid guide.

## Quick Win You Can Do Today

Take your current hero photo. Move your key light (or window) 35° to the side and 20° above. Add a white foam board as a bounce on the opposite side. Shoot from 3/4 angle instead of straight on.

Upload the new version. Watch your conversion rate.
